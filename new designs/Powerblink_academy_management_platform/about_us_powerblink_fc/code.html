About the Academy

Our Story

Rooted in the heart of Ibeju Lekki, Powerblink Football Club Limited is more than an academy—it's a high-performance ecosystem dedicated to transforming raw potential into world-class athletic excellence. We are building the next generation of football leaders.

target

Mission

To discover, develop, mentor, and showcase young football talent through structured coaching, ensuring every player reaches their professional ceiling.

Vision

To be Nigeria's most professional youth football academy, recognized globally for producing elite athletes and disciplined leaders on and off the pitch.

Our Journey

Academy Launch
